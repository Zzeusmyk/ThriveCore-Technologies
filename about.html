Our Story

Building Africa's Digital Future

ThriveCore Technologies Nigeria Limited is a premier information technology solutions provider established in 2015, headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria.

As a dynamic and innovative IT company, we specialize in cutting-edge software development, comprehensive ICT services, cybersecurity solutions, and enterprise hardware configuration. Our team of highly skilled professionals delivers exceptional technological solutions that enable businesses across Nigeria and West Africa to optimize operations, secure digital assets, and achieve sustainable growth.

With a proven track record of serving over 100 clients across financial services, healthcare, education, oil and gas, manufacturing, and government sectors, ThriveCore Technologies has established itself as a trusted technology partner.
