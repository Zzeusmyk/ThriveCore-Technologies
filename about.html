Our story

Your trusted technology partner.

ThriveCore Technologies Nigeria Limited is a premier IT solutions provider established in 2015 and headquartered in Abuja. We specialise in software development, comprehensive ICT services, cybersecurity, and enterprise hardware configuration.

Our team of skilled professionals delivers solutions that help businesses across Nigeria and West Africa optimise operations, secure digital assets, and grow sustainably. With 100+ clients across financial services, healthcare, education, oil & gas, manufacturing, and government, we’ve become a technology partner organisations rely on.
